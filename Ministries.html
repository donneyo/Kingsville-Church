Ministry overview
Encounter with Grace

This is the radio ministry of the church. Currently we air this ten minutes program on City F.m 105.1 in Lagos Nigeria every Saturday and Sunday. We believe God to increase the number of stations as we are enabled.


Men On Fire Conference

This is a quarterly gathering of men in the church. Here men minister to one another and share on relevant issues to men that they may excel in Christ-like manhood.

K.E.E.P

This is the outreach of the church that takes care of the school fees of those who are unable to pay on their own or the parents cannot afford to pay.
